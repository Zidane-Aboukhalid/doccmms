title: How to Edit or Delete an Asset
---

## Edit an Asset
You can update an asset profile at any time to maintain database accuracy.

1.  Navigate to **Facilities and Assets > Assets**.
2.  Click the **More (⋯)** icon on the far right of the asset's row.
3.  Select **Edit**, modify the necessary fields, and click **Submit**.

---

## Delete an Asset
Follow these steps to permanently remove an equipment profile.

1.  Navigate to **Facilities and Assets > Assets**.
2.  Click the **More (⋯)** icon and select **Delete** (Trash icon).

:::warning System Warning
Deletion is permanent. The system will prevent deletion if the asset is linked to existing records (e.g., work orders). Consider marking as **Retired** instead.
:::

3.  Confirm the deletion in the prompt.
