# Facilities & Assets

The **Facilities & Assets** module is where you manage everything you maintain. From large physical locations down to granular equipment and tools, this module keeps a complete digital record of your operational infrastructure.

---

## Sub-Modules

Select a section to manage your assets:

*   [**Locations**](/docs/assets/locations/about) — Set up your facility hierarchy (Sites, Buildings, Rooms).
*   [**Equipment**](/docs/assets/equipment/about) — Track individual machines, machinery types, and maintenance history.
*   [**Tools**](/docs/assets/tools/about) — Manage shared assets or portable equipment that requires tracking.
*   [**Parts Consumption**](/docs/docs/parts-and-supplies/about) — *Note: Parts are managed separately but can be linked to any asset.*

---

## Why Asset Tracking?

By properly setting up your assets, you can:
1.  **Track Costs**: See exactly how much maintenance is spent on specific machines.
2.  **Monitor Health**: View meter readings and trigger history.
3.  **Audit History**: Keep a full record of all work orders performed on an asset.
