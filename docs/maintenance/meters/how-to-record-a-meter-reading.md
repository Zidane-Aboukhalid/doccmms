## How to Record a Meter Reading

> **Required Permission:** Manage Meter Readings

There are **two primary methods** to manually enter a new reading for an existing meter.

### Step 1: Access the Input Form

**Method 1: Quick Add via List View**
1. Navigate to **Maintenance > Meters**.
2. Locate the specific meter in the list.
3. Click the **Reading** icon associated with that meter's row.

**Method 2: Add via Detail View**
1. Navigate to **Maintenance > Meters**.
2. Click on the target meter to open its detail view.
3. Click the **Reading** button located within the interface.

### Step 2: Record the Data
Complete the data entry form. The system will immediately evaluate the new data against any active Meter Triggers.

| Field | Instruction | Required |
|---|---|---|
| **Meter** | The system pre-defines this field based on your selection. It cannot be changed. | ✅ System |
| **Reading Value** | Enter the current numerical data from the equipment. | ✅ Yes |
| **Note** | Log any visual observations or context regarding the reading. | — |

### Step 3: Confirm Entry
Click the **Submit** button to append the new reading to the meter's historical log.

:::note
Editing recorded meters requires the **Allow Backdated Meter History Editing** permission.
:::
