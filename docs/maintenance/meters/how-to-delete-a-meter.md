## How to Delete a Meter

> **Required Permission:** Delete Meter

Deleting a meter **permanently removes it** from the system.

:::danger Warning
Deleting a meter is a **destructive action**. It may permanently erase all historical reading data associated with it and disable any active Meter Triggers or Preventive Maintenance schedules relying on its data.
:::

### Step 1: Locate the Meter
Navigate to **Maintenance > Meters**. Use the search bar or filters to find the meter you intend to delete.

### Step 2: Initiate Deletion
Select the specific meter from the list. Click the **Delete** button located in the action column or within the meter's detail view.

### Step 3: Confirm Deletion
A confirmation prompt will appear to prevent accidental data loss. Review the warning and click **Delete** to permanently remove the meter and its associated data from the system.
