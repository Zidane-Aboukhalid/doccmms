# Meters

## About Meters

The **Meters** module serves as a historical tracking tool used to record physical conditions or operational metrics over time. It allows maintenance teams to log continuous numerical data based on a specific unit of measurement (such as degrees Celsius, kilometers, or PSI).

### How It Works

A Meter creates a **chronological data timeline** for any monitored variable. As technicians perform routine checks, they log new numerical readings at different points in time.

**Example:**
> You create a Meter to track the ambient temperature of a critical cold storage room.
> - **Monday**: a technician logs a normal baseline reading of **-18°C**.
> - **Thursday**: a routine check results in a new reading of **-5°C**.
>
> By looking at this simple timeline, a manager can instantly spot the sudden rise in temperature and investigate the cooling system before inventory is ruined.

### The Operational Value

By continuously adding new readings to a Meter, you build a precise, trackable history of the monitored condition. This empowers maintenance teams to:

- Monitor real-time operational metrics across the facility.
- Quickly identify **abnormal trends**, irregular spikes, or sudden drops in data that could indicate a developing operational problem.
