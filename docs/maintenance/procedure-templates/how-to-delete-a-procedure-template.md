# How to Delete a Procedure Template

Follow these steps to permanently remove an SOP or checklist from your system.

1. Navigate to **Maintenance > Procedure Templates**.
2. Locate the procedure. Click the **More (⋯)** icon in the far-right column.
3. Click the **Delete** option (trash bin icon).

:::danger Critical Warning
Deleting a Procedure Template is a **permanent action**.
- **Future Impact**: This template will no longer be available to attach to new Work Orders.
- **Historical Data**: Deleting the master template does **not** erase data from Work Orders already completed using this SOP — your past compliance records remain intact.
:::

4. Confirm the action in the confirmation dialog to permanently remove the template.
