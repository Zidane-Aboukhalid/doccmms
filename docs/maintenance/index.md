# Maintenance

The **Maintenance** module is the operational core of UniCMMS. It centralizes every tool your team needs to manage, track, and automate all maintenance activities.

---

## Sub-Modules

Select a section to learn more:

* [**Work Orders**](/docs/maintenance/work-orders/about) — Create, assign, and track maintenance tasks.
* [**Work Requests**](/docs/maintenance/work-requests/about) — Submit and review maintenance tickets.
* [**Regular Maintenance (PM)**](/docs/maintenance/regular-maintenance/about) — Schedule preventive maintenance plans.
* [**Procedure Templates**](/docs/maintenance/procedure-templates/about) — Build standardized SOPs and checklists.
* [**Meters**](/docs/maintenance/meters/about) — Track physical conditions and operational metrics.
* [**Triggers**](/docs/maintenance/triggers/about) — Automate workflows with condition-based rules.
