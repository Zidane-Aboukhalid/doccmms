## Change Asset Status
Update the operational state and record the root cause of downtime.

1.  In the Assets list, click **More (⋯)** and select **Change Status**.
2.  Select the new **Status** and the **Reason**.
3.  Adjust the **Change Timestamp** and add optional notes.
4.  Click **Submit**.

---

## View Insights (KPIs)
Access real-time performance analytics for a specific piece of equipment.

1.  Click on the asset's **Code** (e.g. AST1) to open its profile.
2.  Select the **Insights** tab from the left menu.
3.  Review metrics like **Uptime**, **MTBF** (Mean Time Between Failures), **MTTR** (Mean Time To Repair), and **Number of Failures**.

---

## Depreciation and Warranties
### Add Depreciation Tracking
1.  In the asset profile, navigate to the **Depreciation** tab and click **+ Add**.
2.  Enter **Purchase Cost**, **Useful Life**, **Residual Value**, and **Purchase Date**.
3.  Click **Submit** to generate the schedule.

### Add Asset Warranties
1.  Navigate to the **Warranties** tab and click **+ Add**.
2.  Define expiration: by **Date** or by **Condition** (e.g. after a certain number of run hours).
3.  Enter expiration details, certificate number, and provider.
4.  Click **Submit**.
