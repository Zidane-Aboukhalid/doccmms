About UniCMMS​

UniCMMS is a cloud-based CMMS (Computerized Maintenance Management System) for web and mobile. It centralizes work orders, assets, inventory, and preventive maintenance into a single platform — giving your maintenance team everything they need in one place.

How the Platform Works​

UniCMMS is organized around a few core concepts:

1. Assets & Equipment​

Everything in UniCMMS starts with your assets — machines, equipment, or facilities you need to maintain. Each asset has its own profile, history, and linked work orders.

2. Work Orders​

A Work Order is the central engine of your maintenance operations. It authorizes, details, and tracks a specific maintenance task from start to finish — whether it's a corrective repair or a scheduled inspection.

3. Preventive Maintenance​

Regular Maintenance (PM) schedules allow you to automate recurring maintenance tasks. You can trigger them based on time (every Monday) or meter readings (every 500 hours).

4. Meters & Triggers​

Meters let you record continuous numerical data (temperature, pressure, hours). Triggers watch those meter readings and automatically fire actions when thresholds are crossed.

5. Inventory & Parts​

UniCMMS tracks your spare parts inventory. When a work order is completed, the system automatically deducts the consumed parts from your stock.

Role-Based Access​

UniCMMS uses a role-based permission system. Different users see different features depending on their role:

RoleTypical Capabilities
System AdministratorFull access — configure tenants, users, permissions
Maintenance ManagerManage work orders, approve requests, access reports
TechnicianExecute work orders, log meter readings, complete procedures
RequesterSubmit work requests only (restricted access)
